High-of-day and low-of-day reversal strategy review.

PlayMaker helps traders review where price could react near high-of-day and low-of-day areas, then compare confluence and risk before deciding whether the setup is worth attention.

High-of-day reactions

Review whether price is rejecting, sweeping, or continuing near the high instead of chasing the candle without a plan.

Low-of-day reactions

Study low-of-day reaction areas for possible reversal behavior, stop planning, and cleaner limit entry review.

Confluence strength

Compare the level against other stack areas so a reversal idea has more context than price alone.

Built for review, not guessing

High-of-day and low-of-day reversal strategy work requires structure. PlayMaker gives the setup a place to live, with notes, direction, grading, and journal results connected to the same workflow.
