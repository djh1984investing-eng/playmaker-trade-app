Futures trading signals organized for NQ reversal setups.

PlayMaker keeps futures trading signals, setup cards, direction, confluence, and journal notes in one workflow so traders can review the level before reacting.

Signal cards

Each setup can be reviewed by price, direction, confluence, and risk plan so the trade idea is easier to understand.

Reversal context

Signals are most useful when they are reviewed against high-of-day, low-of-day, session deviation, and other reaction zones.

Manual grading

Manual setup review helps compare a planned entry against nearby levels and directional bias before execution.

After-trade review

Journal results help traders study whether a reversal setup followed the plan or exposed a repeat mistake.
