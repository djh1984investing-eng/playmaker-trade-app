How PlayMaker helps organize the trading day.

PlayMaker gives futures traders one place to review NQ futures reversal setups, manage order ideas, and study the results afterward.

1

Review the active setup board

Scan current long and short ideas by price so important futures market levels, including high-of-day and low-of-day reactions, are easier to compare.

2

Check confluence and entry planning

Use the workflow to review the direction, nearby levels, stop size, and the strength of the setup before taking action on a possible market top or bottom.

3

Track fills and management

Move filled ideas into the management area, keep notes, and track whether the setup is still behaving as expected.

4

Journal the final result

Record wins, losses, break-even trades, max move, drawdown, and notes so progress can be reviewed over time.
